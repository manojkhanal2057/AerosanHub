What We Deliver

Six pillars of the
Aerosan system.

From smart IoT monitoring to menstrual hygiene management and waste-to-energy technology — every Aerosan service integrates into a single self-sustaining urban sanitation platform.

01
Hygienic Smart Facilities

Aerosan HUBs maintain consistently clean, safe conditions through touchless fixtures, automated cleaning alerts, and real-time sensor data — eliminating the reactive maintenance cycle that causes most public toilet failures in Nepal.

  • ✓
    Automated occupancy-based cleaning triggers
  • ✓
    Touchless taps, dispensers, and flush systems
  • ✓
    Air quality and odor monitoring sensors
  • ✓
    Operator alert push notifications via mobile

02
Gender-Friendly & MHM Inclusive Design

Every Aerosan HUB is designed with women and girls at the centre — dedicated female sections, menstrual hygiene management (MHM) infrastructure, and safety features that make a real difference to daily life and dignity.

  • ✓
    Dedicated women-only section with secure entry
  • ✓
    Sanitary vending machines and hygienic disposal bins
  • ✓
    Privacy screens, clean changing facilities
  • ✓
    Full wheelchair access, baby-care stations, safety lighting
  • ✓
    Emergency call buttons and well-lit pathways

CleanTech
03
Waste-to-Energy Technology

Sealed anaerobic bio-digesters convert 100% of organic waste into biogas for on-site energy and certified organic fertilizer sold to local agricultural markets — protecting the Bagmati and Bishnumati rivers from ever receiving another drop of sewage.

  • ✓
    Sealed bio-digester — zero open drainage
  • ✓
    Biogas used for on-site lighting and cooking
  • ✓
    Certified organic fertilizer for partner farms
  • ✓
    Full Bagmati river pollution elimination per HUB
