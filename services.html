What We Deliver

Six pillars of the
Aerosan system.

From smart IoT monitoring to menstrual hygiene management and waste‑to‑energy — every service integrates into one self‑sustaining urban sanitation platform.

01

Hygienic Smart Facilities

Touchless fixtures, automated cleaning alerts, and real‑time sensor data — eliminating the reactive maintenance cycle.

  • ✓ Automated occupancy‑based cleaning
  • ✓ Touchless taps & flush systems
  • ✓ Air quality monitoring
02

Gender‑Friendly & MHM

Dedicated female sections, sanitary vending, and safety features that make a real difference to daily dignity.

  • ✓ Women‑only secure entry
  • ✓ Sanitary vending & disposal
  • ✓ Full wheelchair access
03

Waste‑to‑Energy Tech

Sealed bio‑digesters convert 100% of waste into biogas for energy and certified organic fertilizer.

  • ✓ Zero river discharge
  • ✓ Biogas for on‑site use
  • ✓ Fertilizer for local farms
04

IoT Monitoring Dashboard

Real‑time web dashboard tracking occupancy, cleanliness scores, energy production, and maintenance.

  • ✓ Live occupancy analytics
  • ✓ Biogas output metering
  • ✓ Municipal reporting
05

Site Design & Planning

Urban site selection, architectural design, and infrastructure integration for new HUB deployments.

  • ✓ Site feasibility studies
  • ✓ Architectural blueprints
  • ✓ Utility integration
06

Impact Reporting

Quarterly social, environmental, and financial impact reports for donors, investors, and government.

  • ✓ Social impact metrics
  • ✓ Environmental savings
  • ✓ Financial transparency
