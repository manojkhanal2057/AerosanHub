What Drives Us

Our values

Dignity First
Every design decision starts with one question: does this preserve the dignity of the person using it?
MHM as Standard
Menstrual hygiene management infrastructure is not optional. It is foundational to gender-equitable public space.
Circular Thinking
Waste is a resource in the wrong place. We engineer systems that recover value from every material flow.
Gender Equity
Safe sanitation for women is foundational to public life and economic participation — not an afterthought.
Financial Accountability
We refuse to build systems that collapse without subsidies. Every HUB must sustain itself financially.
Community Ownership
HUBs succeed when the community around them owns the outcome — our cooperative model ensures exactly this.
