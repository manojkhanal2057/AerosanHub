Featured Initiative

Waste-to-Value Circular Economy

⚡ Active — 2024 to present
Circular Economy · CleanTech
Connecting HUB outputs to urban food systems and energy markets

The Waste-to-Value project maps biogas and organic fertilizer output from all active Aerosan HUBs to verified buyers — restaurants, institutional kitchens, and peri-urban farms. Every gram of waste becomes a product. Nothing reaches the river.
