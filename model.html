The Aerosan HUB Model

Infrastructure that
pays for itself.

A systemic approach addressing the root causes of urban sanitation failure — built on circular economics, smart technology, and human dignity.

Problem vs Solution

What's broken. How we fix it.

⚠ The ProblemUrban Sanitation Today
  • ✕
    Less than 30% of public toilets remain functional in Nepal's cities
  • ✕
    Unsafe, unhygienic conditions that undermine human dignity and public health
  • ✕
    No menstrual hygiene facilities — women face dangerous conditions monthly
  • ✕
    No safe, women-accessible sanitation forcing open defecation
  • ✕
    Raw sewage discharged into the Bagmati, Bishnumati and other rivers
  • ✕
    No viable maintenance funding — facilities collapse without subsidy
✦ Aerosan SolutionThe HUB Model
  • ✓
    Smart, IoT-monitored facilities with real-time cleanliness and maintenance alerts
  • ✓
    Female-dedicated sections with sanitary dispensers, disposal bins, and privacy
  • ✓
    Menstrual Hygiene Management (MHM) built into design — not added as an afterthought
  • ✓
    Disabled-accessible stalls, baby-care stations, and enhanced safety lighting
  • ✓
    Anaerobic bio-digestion — 100% waste to biogas and organic fertilizer, zero river discharge
  • ✓
    Blended revenue model sustains operations with zero government dependency
Core Pillars

Six pillars of every HUB

Technology, equity, and circular economics integrated into one self-sustaining platform.

01

Hygienic Smart Facilities

Touchless fixtures, automated cleaning alerts, air-quality sensors, and odor monitoring — every operating hour.

02

MHM-Friendly Design

Sanitary vending, hygienic disposal bins, privacy screens, and changing facilities in every women's section.

03

Gender & Disability Inclusive

Dedicated female sections, full wheelchair access, baby-care, safety lighting — standard in all HUBs.

04

Waste-to-Energy

Sealed bio-digesters convert 100% of waste to biogas and organic fertilizer. Zero river discharge — by design.

05

IoT Monitoring Dashboard

Live occupancy, cleanliness, energy output, and maintenance metrics for operators and municipalities.

06

Revenue-Generating Model

Five income streams create a reliable surplus — fees, biogas, fertilizer, advertising, training licences.
